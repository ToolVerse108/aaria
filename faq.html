AARIA — Buyer & Investor FAQs

General FAQs

1. What documents should I check before buying a plot? ▸
Check title chain, Encumbrance Certificate (EC), mutation, seller ID proof, layout approvals and ensure no legal disputes exist.
2. What is circle rate? ▸
Circle rate is the government-declared minimum value for registering land. It affects stamp duty and tax calculations.
3. How do I know if a plot is legally safe? ▸
A plot is legally safe if title is clean, approvals are valid, EC is clear, and AARIA’s document grade scores it as Standard/Premium.

Investment FAQs

4. What is a good ROI in plot investment? ▸
Long-term land bankers target 10–20% CAGR. Flippers aim for higher short-term returns but with higher risk.
5. How long should I hold a plot? ▸
2–5 years for flippers; 5–10+ years for long-term investors. Infrastructure development greatly impacts timing.
6. How do I estimate future growth? ▸
Use comparable sales, infra plans, market cycle and circle-rate gaps. AARIA’s forecast engine provides realistic growth bands.

Loan & Finance FAQs

7. Should I take a loan or buy with cash? ▸
Loans help leverage returns if growth beats interest. Cash avoids EMI burden and reduces risk.
8. How is EMI calculated? ▸
Using amortization formula (Principal + Interest). AARIA auto-calculates EMI instantly for buyers and investors.

NRI FAQs

9. Can NRIs buy plots in India? ▸
Yes, under FEMA rules. NRIs can buy residential and commercial plots but agricultural land has restrictions.
10. How does repatriation of sale proceeds work? ▸
Repatriation requires documentation, tax clearance, and a verified inward remittance trail. Consult a CA for compliance.

Tax FAQs

11. When does long-term capital gains apply? ▸
After 24 months of holding. LTCG offers indexation benefits and lower tax rates.
12. What are typical selling costs? ▸
Brokerage, legal fees, transfer charges, and TDS adjustments based on seller/residency.
