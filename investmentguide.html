AARIA — Investment Guide

1. Investor Profile

1

Identify Who You Are

Plot Buyer, Land Banker, NRI or Flipper — each requires different expectations, risk levels and time horizons.

2. Location Validation Checklist

  • Access to highways and utilities
  • Upcoming infrastructure projects
  • Zoning and permitted land use
  • Market vs circle rate trends

3. Legal & Documentation Scorecard

Check title chain, EC, mutation, and seller identity. Clear documentation accelerates exit and lowers risk.

4. Financial Modeling

Include market growth, taxes, loan interest, holding cost and selling cost. AARIA calculator handles these automatically.

5. Risk Matrix

  • Document Grade: Premium / Standard / Issues
  • Market Cycle: Soft / Neutral / Rising
  • Sales Velocity: Slow / Moderate / Fast
  • Macro Economy: Interest rates & liquidity

6. Tax Notes (India)

  • Long-term capital gains apply after 24 months
  • Stamp duty & registration count in acquisition cost
  • TDS applicable in many cases
  • NRI repatriation requires proper documentation

7. Investor Playbooks

  • Land Banker — buy early near infra projects
  • Flipper — undervalued, fast-moving plots
  • NRI — focus on repatriation & compliance
  • Developer Partner — aggregation & feasibility

  • Auto ROI/IRR/CAGR scenario builder
  • Legal grade classifier
  • Growth multipliers & comparables
  • Investor-ready exportable reports
